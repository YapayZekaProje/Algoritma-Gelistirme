GreedySearchForMap1'a yorum satırlarının eklenmiş halidir.

After adding GreedySearch script:
Click on the object that contains the GreedySearch script
In the Inspector window:
You will see the Seeker and Target public fields in the script.
Drag a Seeker object from the Hierarchy window to the Seeker field.
Drag a Target object to the Target field.
